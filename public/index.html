Grid Paper Templates

Your home for all printable grid paper and graph paper templates, including for 2D pixel art and 3D voxel art. More grids coming soon!

These templates are also ideal for sketching ideas for pixel art, favicons, and voxel art. 32x32 represents metaverse blocks for Minecraft, The Sandbox, and others. 32x64 grid paper can be used for character design in games.

You are free to print as much as you want.
